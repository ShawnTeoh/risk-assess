Introduction

SAfER (Strategies Analysis for Enhancing Resilience) helps you perform the following:

  1. Identify the range of human performance strategies that workers could or should use within a work system.
  2. Identify the design changes needed to do the following:
    1. Promote strategies that lead to successful outcomes.
    2. Prevent strategies that lead to unsuccessful outcomes.
